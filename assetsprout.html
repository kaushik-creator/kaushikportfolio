AssetSprout

A mobile investment app designed to make wealth creation accessible to first-time investors in India — demystifying stocks, mutual funds, and portfolio management.

The Brief

The founder (a financial advisor) kept saying: "People want to invest but they're terrified of losing money. They don't know where to start." He brought me on for 6 months to design an app that would change that.

The hard part wasn't making it pretty. It was making it trustworthy enough that a 25-year-old would hand us their money. That's a different kind of design problem.

Research
What Actually Stops People From Investing

I interviewed 15 people. Most said the same thing: "I'll lose everything." Not because they were dumb. They genuinely didn't understand what risk meant. One guy said he tried an app once, bought a stock, it went down 2%, and he panicked and sold at a loss. Then he never invested again.

So the app couldn't just show pretty charts. It had to teach people what volatility actually is, help them build a plan, and show them they weren't making a casino bet.
